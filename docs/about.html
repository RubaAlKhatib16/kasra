Our Vision for Ethical Payments

Kasra was born from a simple idea: financial freedom shouldn't come at the cost of ethical principles. We're building a future where payments are transparent, flexible, and accessible to all.

Our Journey

2019

The Idea

Founded by a team of fintech and blockchain veterans who saw the need for ethical payment solutions in the MENA region.

2020

Sharia Certification

Received full Sharia compliance certification from leading Islamic finance scholars.

2021

Hedera Integration

Became one of the first BNPL platforms to leverage Hedera Hashgraph for transparent transactions.

2023

Regional Expansion

Launched operations in 5 countries across the Middle East, serving over 100,000 customers.
