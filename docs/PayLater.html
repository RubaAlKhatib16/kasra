Questions You Might Ask

Find answers to the most common questions about Kasra Pay Later.

Is Kasra really interest-free?

Yes, absolutely! Kasra operates on a Sharia-compliant model with no interest charges. You only pay the original product price divided into convenient installments.

What happens if I miss a payment?

Kasra doesn't charge late fees. However, consistent missed payments may affect your credit limit and future purchasing power. We send friendly reminders to help you stay on track.

How do I check my remaining balance?

You can easily check your remaining balance, upcoming payments, and transaction history through your Kasra dashboard at any time.

Can I pay off early?

Yes! You can pay off your balance early at any time without any prepayment penalties. Early repayment may even improve your credit score with Kasra.

Is Kasra approved by Islamic finance guidelines?

Yes, Kasra has been reviewed and approved by Islamic financial scholars. Our model is fully compliant with Sharia principles, with no riba (interest) involved in any transactions.
